SEO(Search Engine Optimization)

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a digital marketing strategy aimed at increasing a website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) through paid advertising.
It involves creating and optimizing ads that appear on search engine results pages when users search for specific keywords related to the advertiser's products or services.
SEM typically encompasses both Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising, where advertisers pay each time their ad is clicked, and Search Engine Optimization (SEO),
SEM allows businesses to target specific audiences, increase website traffic, which focuses on improving a website's organic search engine rankings, and generate leads or sales by placing ads strategically within search engine results.
